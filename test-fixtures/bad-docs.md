# User Guide

Hey guys! Welcome to our app. This guide will help you get started.

## Getting Started

It's super simple to use. Just follow these easy steps:

1. Click the login button
2. See the dashboard below
3. Obviously, you'll want to configure your settings first

## User Profiles

Each user has a profile with the following fields:

- **Name**: First name and last name (e.g., John Smith, Jane Doe)
- **Gender**: Male or Female (required)
- **Marital Status**: Single, Married, Divorced
- **Spouse**: Your husband or wife's name
- **Mother's Name**: For security questions
- **Father's Name**: For security questions

## Account Settings

### Address

Enter your home address:
- Street
- City
- State (required)
- ZIP Code (5 digits)

### Payment

Add your credit card to enable purchases. We accept Visa, Mastercard, and American Express.

## Holiday Features

### Christmas Mode

Enable festive themes during the holiday season! Your dashboard will show:
- Christmas countdown
- Holiday greeting: "Merry Christmas!"
- Special offers on holiday meals like turkey and ham

### Birthday Celebrations

We'll automatically send birthday wishes to all your contacts. Everyone loves birthday surprises!

## Accessibility

Our app is designed to be simple enough that even your grandmother can use it. Digital natives will find it intuitive, while older users might need some help from younger family members.

## Troubleshooting

If something goes wrong, don't go crazy! Just:

1. Do a sanity check on your inputs
2. Make sure you're not being dumb about the requirements
3. The fix is usually blindingly obvious

## Contact Support

Reach out to our team of businessmen and policemen who can help resolve your issues. Our chairman reviews all escalations personally.
