# Inclusive Language Reference

Quick reference for non-inclusive terms and alternatives. Source: Charter §8.8

## Gendered Language
| Instead of... | Use... | Why |
|--------------|--------|-----|
| `guys` | `everyone`, `folks`, `people`, `team` | Gendered term |
| `he/she`, `his/her` | `they`, `their` | Excludes non-binary identities |
| `mankind` | `humanity`, `people` | Gendered term |
| `manpower` | `workforce`, `staffing` | Gendered term |
| `man-hours` | `person-hours`, `work hours` | Gendered term |
| `fireman`, `policeman`, `chairman` | `firefighter`, `police officer`, `chairperson` | Gendered job titles |
| `mother/father` | `parent`, `caregiver` | Excludes diverse family structures |
| `husband/wife` | `partner`, `spouse` | Excludes diverse relationships |

## Ableist Language
| Instead of... | Use... | Why |
|--------------|--------|-----|
| `crazy`, `insane` | specific description | Stigmatizes mental health |
| `OCD` (casual use) | `meticulous`, `detail-oriented` | Trivializes condition |
| `blind to` | `unaware of`, `overlooking` | Ableist metaphor |
| `deaf to` | `ignoring`, `dismissing` | Ableist metaphor |
| `crippled`, `lame` | `broken`, `impaired`, `limited` | Ableist slurs |
| `sanity check` | `review`, `verify`, `check` | Stigmatizes mental health |
| `dumb` (as in `dumb down`) | `simplify` | Ableist term |

## Racially Problematic Terms
| Instead of... | Use... | Why |
|--------------|--------|-----|
| `whitelist` | `allowlist` | Racial connotations |
| `blacklist` | `blocklist`, `denylist` | Racial connotations |
| `master` (branch/device) | `main`, `primary`, `leader` | Slavery connotations |
| `slave` (device/process) | `replica`, `follower`, `secondary` | Slavery connotations |
| `grandfathered` | `legacy`, `exempted`, `pre-existing` | Racist origins (Jim Crow) |

## Ageist Language
| Instead of... | Use... | Why |
|--------------|--------|-----|
| `elderly`, `seniors` | `older adults`, `adults aged X+` | Patronizing |
| `digital natives` | `people familiar with technology` | Ageist assumption |
| `young people these days` | specific, non-judgmental description | Ageist generalization |

## Classist & Other Problematic Terms
| Instead of... | Use... | Why |
|--------------|--------|-----|
| `low-income`, `disadvantaged` | specific context or avoid labels | Stigmatizing |
| `uneducated` | `without formal education in X` | Dismissive |
| `illegal` (person) | `undocumented person` | Dehumanizing |
| `third world` | `Global South`, specific countries | Colonial terminology |
| `normal user` | `typical user`, `most users` | Implies others are "abnormal" |

## Ability-Assuming Verbs
| Instead of... | Use... | Why |
|--------------|--------|-----|
| `click here` | `select this link` | Not all users click |
| `see below` | `read below`, `the following` | Not all users see |
| `simple`, `just`, `easy` | describe actual steps | Subjective difficulty |
| `obviously` | omit or explain | Assumes shared knowledge |
